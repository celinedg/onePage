From a Designer

Working as a production manager for a small fashion brand in Paris, I had to use tools like Excel to follow our production. There are better suited programmes out there, but the cost is quite high and they can be difficult to learn. So I decided to build one for you!

Young designers or fashion brands that want a useful and modern tool at a very reasonable price per month. You don’t have to engage in a long term contract for a minimum of a year, for example. You pay every month as long as you need it! Sounds fair?
